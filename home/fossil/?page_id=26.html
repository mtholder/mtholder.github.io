Software

  • DPPDiv – Bayesian estimation of species divergence times under a Dirichlet process prior
  • FossilGen – A program for simulating phylogenies and fossil occurrence times under the serial-sampled birth-death process
  • PhyPatClassProb – development/testing application for algorithms for calculating the probability of classes of data patterns on trees. These algorithms are used in corrections for ascertainment biases.
