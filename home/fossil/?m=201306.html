Talks coming up at Evolution and iEvoBio

Posted on June 3, 2013 by Mark Holder

Last week, we had our first meeting in lovely Lawrence, KS.  We’ll try to keep this site fairly up-to-date. If you are going to the Evolution meetings in June, be on the look out for Tracy and me.

Tracy’s talk is Sunday ( program link ). She’ll be talking some very nice work that she has been doing with John Huelsenbeck and Tanja Stadler about better ways of attaching nodes that represent dated fossils to phylogenetic trees. I will be giving a lightning talk at iEvoBio , which is a very fun meeting that is held on the last day of the Evolution meetings + the day after the main meeting.

Update on June 7, 2013: The iEvoBio schedule is now available .
